I am also an avid movie enthusiast. After a class I took while at Bellarmine College Prep called Film Composition and Literature, I began to appreciate aspects of dialogue, cinematography, plots, and score. Since then, I have watched countless movies, and have developed a unique liking for Italian mob movies (Godfather, Goodfellas) as well as mind-bending thrillers (The Usual Suspect). I am planning to build out the portion of this website dedicated to film with some more of my thoughts and reviews of movies that I have seen. For now, I will list out some of my favorite movies.
